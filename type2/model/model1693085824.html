INTRODUCTION

Dimmigration Services is an immigration firm that is experienced in helping you immigrate to Canada, by suggesting the best immigration program that fits your qualifications as well as helping you with the process ensuring that your application is complete and meets the approval criteria. ...We are able to help you whether you are in Canada or outside. Having experience in different kinds of temporary and permanent applications gives us the ability to help you no matter what your case is. We are regulated by the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulated Council and authorized by the government of Canada to offer immigration services under the authorisation number: R709 716. Dimmigration Services could help you choose the right immigration program during the consultation session, moreover, we will help you throughout the process until approval. We also provide new immigrants with all the needed resources that help them settle and integrate into Canadian society. We will represent you with Citizenship and Immigration Canada and the Immigration Board of Canada no matter what kind of application you decide to apply. We will help in reuniting families by applying to family sponsorship programs or visitor visas. Moreover, we help in initiating inland refugee claims, applying for student & work permits, and completing express entry applications. Choosing our services will give you the opportunity to know what all your options are, choose your best route, and finalise your application professionally and efficiently.
